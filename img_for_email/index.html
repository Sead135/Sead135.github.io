Chef Jeff’s Recipe of the Month
OMELETTE PIE
Ingredients
  • 7 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup of 2% or whole milk
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 2 cups of vegetables. Slice the following into small chunks: onions (red and/or white), bell peppers (red and green), jalapeño pepper, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, broccoli florettes, and mushrooms.
  • 1 cup (or more) of blended cheeses of your choice (we use Organic Valley Thick Cut Shredded 3 Cheese Mexican Blend)
Directions
Step 1
Preheat oven to 400°F (204°C).
Step 2
Using a large baking dish (we use a 9 1/2 inch Pyrex pie plate), coat entire inside of dish with butter (can use a paper towel to apply butter). Alternatively, you can use parchment paper to line the baking dish.
Step 3
Mix eggs, milk and sea salt in large mixing container or blender. Then, add vegetables and cheese.
Step 4
Pour into baking dish. Bake in the oven at 400°F (204°C) for 25-30 minutes. Make sure it is fully cooked.
Step 5
Serve with your choice of salsa and/or hot sauce.
